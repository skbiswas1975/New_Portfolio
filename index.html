About me

Hello, my name is Sanjib.

I am a Kansas based full stack web developer. I am trained in the stack (Mongo, Express, React, & Node). I also have experience with creating front end designs using Bootstrap, JQuery, and HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

I learned to code at KU Edwards campus and I've also had the opportunity to work for various places for many years. These experiences have given me both technical proficiency as a developer as well as an understanding of the importance of working in a great team culture.

My dream is to make great software, websites, databases, games, etc. with great people.

Please contact me if you'd like to discuss the possibility of working together.
